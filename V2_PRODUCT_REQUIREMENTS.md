# T&K Photo V2 Product Requirements

Last updated: 2026-05-04
Branch: `v2-upload`

## Current V2 Goals

- Support Chinese and English language switching across the public exhibition and the private studio.
- Support Chinese-first upload and management flows in the V2 admin dashboard.
- Support photo notes, short essays, and broader portfolio management in the studio.
- Preserve the restrained, exhibition-style visual direction established by V1.

## Product Notes

- The public exhibition remains visually stable and continues to use the static data source until the later migration phase.
- The studio continues to evolve in phased steps so V1 stays safe while V2 management capabilities grow.
- Language switching should stay lightweight and maintainable, using a small dictionary-based approach rather than a heavy framework.
